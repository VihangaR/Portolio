About Me

My name is Vihanga Ratnasinghe and I love technology. I've been tinkering with computers for as long as I can remember. From building my first computer, to my first program, and attending university for a degree, it has all revolved around technology. I currently attend University of Toronto and am studying in the Machine Learning and Data Mining stream.
I love playing around with new technology, from front-end frameworks such as Bulma, Bootstrap or even Google's very own Materialize design, to back-end technologies such as Node and different databases. I also enjoy the design aspect of it all. I like to create things that are very minimal, but very impactful. Due to that, I believe the quote "More is less" is something that a lot of people should follow.
